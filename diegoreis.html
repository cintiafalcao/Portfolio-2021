Diego Reis Advocacia

Branding

2014

Brand identity and logo concept for Reis Advocacia e Consultoria Jurídica, a Brazilian law firm.

What I Did

  • Designed brand identity;
  • Created brand collaterals.

For Whom

Reis Advocacia e Consultoria Jurídica specializes in Consumer, Civil, Family and Administrative Law.

Logo

The idea was to get away from the classic icons related to law offices, such as the balance, and instead use the arches, subtly reference the Greek columns and keep it as clean as possible.

Collaterals

Created print collaterals to promote the brand.
