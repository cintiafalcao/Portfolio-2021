Vamos Desenhar

Illustration

2013

Vamos Desenhar is a drawing app idealized by Polo Marte, to teach kids how to draw.

What I Did

  • Designed illustrations;
  • Created animations.

For Whom

Polo Marte is a Brazilian digital agency based in Rio de Janeiro.

My Challenges

Recreate the action of drawing using animations.

    Recreate the Steps

  • Create a simple shape;
  • Sketch the details;
  • Accent lines;
  • Coloring.

Animate with AE

I used Illustrator to redraw all the sketches made with paper and pencil. Then I created the animations in After Effects.
