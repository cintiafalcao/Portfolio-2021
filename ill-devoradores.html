Devoradores de Livros is a website designed to cultivate a consistent reading habit.

What I Did

  • Designed the elements and characters.

For Whom

Devoradores de Livros

My Goal

Create a reading habit. How?

  • Allow children to customize their avatar;
  • Answer the quiz, and get rewarded with new accessories and characters.

How It Works

After reading a book, children look for whether a quiz is already available on the site.

The Quiz is a questionnaire with multiple-choice questions about the book.

After answering the Quiz, they will be virtually rewarded and can customize their avatar.
